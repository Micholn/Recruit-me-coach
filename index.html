About Us

Recruit Me Coach was formed in 2020 and serves as a bridge between college coaches and student-athletes! We have helped hundreds of children throughout the years in the recruiting process obtain scholarships! We believe in equipping student-athletes and their families by educating and guiding them through the recruiting process! We only succeed when our athletes succeed! We work hand in hand with our athletes to make sure they receive exposure to college coaches. We help provide things such as assistance and invitations into a network of coaches for those who are looking to gain valuable spots on college rosters. The primary benefit of going into the college recruiting process with assistance is being able to feel much more confident about it. Through our assessment process, we help eliminate those schools which might not be a good fit for the student-athlete. We help give student-athletes other tools to make an informed decision where to continue their education playing the sport they love!
